## SECTION 1 — What is a Loop and Why Do We Need It?

Imagine you have 100 students and you want to print the name of each student.  
Without a loop you'd write 100 print statements. That's insane.  
A loop lets you write the logic **once** and repeat it automatically until a specific condition is met.

### Without a loop:
```python
print("Areeba")
print("Alishba")
print("Zara")
# ... 97 more lines

With a loop — clean:

students = ["Areeba", "Alishba", "Zara"]
for student in students:
    print(student)

Same result. One piece of logic. Works for 3 or 3 million names.
